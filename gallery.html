Fun Facts About German Shepherds

Hollywood Stars

Famous German Shepherds in movies and TV include Rin Tin Tin, who appeared in 27 Hollywood films and helped save Warner Bros. from bankruptcy.

Presidential Pets

Several U.S. Presidents have owned German Shepherds, including Franklin D. Roosevelt (Major) and John F. Kennedy (Clipper).

By the Numbers

German Shepherds are the 2nd most popular dog breed in the US and can live 9-13 years with proper care.

Super Senses

German Shepherds have about 225 million scent receptors (humans have only 5 million), making them exceptional at detection work.

Global Recognition

Originally called "Alsatian Wolf Dog" in Britain during WWI, the breed is now recognized worldwide by major kennel clubs.
